Breaking News: Bengaluru Metro Expansion

Bangalore is set to further expand its metro network. The Purple and Green Lines will connect new areas across the city, reducing traffic congestion and making commutes easier. The new metro routes will benefit residents in Whitefield, Kengeri, and Bannerghatta Road.

The expansion project is expected to be completed by 2026 and is part of a broader effort to enhance public transportation in the city.
